Frequently asked questions

Is QuickCrop free to use?
Yes, QuickCrop is completely free with no sign-up required. There are no watermarks, no limits, and no hidden fees.
Are my images uploaded to a server?
No. QuickCrop runs entirely in your browser. Your images never leave your device — all cropping is done locally.
What image sizes does QuickCrop support?
QuickCrop includes presets for Instagram Post (1080×1080), LinkedIn Banner (1584×396), Twitter/X Header (1500×500), and YouTube Thumbnail (1280×720). You can also crop to any custom dimensions.

QuickCrop accepts JPG, PNG, and WebP images. All cropped images are exported as high-quality PNG files.

Yes. Click the "Custom" option to enter exact pixel dimensions, use the interactive crop rectangle to select the region, and optionally lock the aspect ratio.
